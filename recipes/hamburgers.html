Hamburger Recipe

Description

This recipe is about hamburgers. I will tell you how to assemble a burger in the most delicious way.

Ingredients

  • Buns
  • Meat
  • Pickles
  • Tomatoes
  • Ketchup
  • Mustard

Steps

  1. Cook the meat
  2. Place seared meat onto bun
  3. Apply the pickles, tomatoes, ketchup, and mustard
  4. Enjoy
